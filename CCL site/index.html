About Us

About Us

Chakamu Construction Limited is a wholly Zambian owned Engineering Company domiciled in Lusaka the capital City Of Zambia. The Company was registered and incorporated in November 2017. Chakamu is a private limitd Company by Shares and registered under the requirements of the compaines' act of the public of Zambia with two directors; the Managing Director being a Technical expert with diverse experience in Zambia Construction Industry for over 8 years and the Director Operations is a Businessman and IT Specialist. Chakamu Construction Limited is Specialised in two major fields of business namely; Construction and Supply with the former being the lead and principle business while the latter acting as abet to the main business.
